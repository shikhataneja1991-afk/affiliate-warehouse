FAQ

Common Questions
Answered Directly.

What wholesaler accounts do I get access to?

TradeZone, Ideal Electrical, and Voltex are currently integrated nationally. Access is through Affiliate Warehouse's corporate accounts — all purchases remain fully job-coded and traceable within each supplier's system.

How does Affiliate Warehouse make money?

We charge 1.1% of the original pre-discount value, billed monthly — only when a verified discount is achieved. If no saving occurs, we invoice nothing. Our incentives are fully aligned with yours.

Am I locked in to any contract?

No. There are no contracts, no minimum spend, no exclusivity, and no penalty for stopping. You can cease participation at any time. Your existing trade accounts remain unaffected.

How do I know pricing is actually improving?

You can compare Affiliate Warehouse pricing against your own trade accounts at any time. Our price tracking system provides a transparent record of what you paid previously versus current pricing.

Is this compliant with supplier systems?

Yes. Affiliate Warehouse operates within existing wholesaler infrastructure. All purchases are job-coded, fully traceable, and processed through normal supplier channels. No terms are circumvented.

What if it stops being useful to me?

Stop using it at any time. No notice period, no exit fees, no consequences. Your trade accounts and supplier relationships remain entirely your own.

How are better prices achieved?

Through aggregated purchasing volume across independent preferred contractors nationally. Combined, smaller businesses access pricing tiers typically available only to large enterprise operators — subject to supplier approval.

Who can apply — is this open to anyone?

No. This is a formal application gateway for existing preferred contractors or businesses seeking preferred contractor status. Applications are reviewed individually. Only approved applicants receive access.
